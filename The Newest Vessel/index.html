Help

Instructions: Click the steam vessel in order to get cargo boxes. Cargo boxes can be used to purchase either different items of trade or allow you to fund more workers. These split parts of the game fall into two distinct categories, idle clicks, and per clicks. The lower you go in the shop, the more expensive it will be, but the more cargo it will provide for you. Idle clicks click for you and give you cargo without you having to do a thing. On the other hand, per clicks give you more cargo every time you click the steam vessel. Your default clicks are 1 cargo per click. You start with no idle clicks as well. The total clicks counter is used to show you when your questions are approaching. The first question starts at 50 clicks. Each question comes after the previous with another 50 clicks to get your question. The 2nd at 100 clicks, 3rd at 150, 4th at 200, and so on until you hit the 32nd question. This question when answered will allow you to win the first part of the game. From this point on you can either choose to continue on to beat the game where you need to get atleast 100 of everything to be considered a winner, or leave because you only came to study. In it's current version of v2.2 the game is meant to be easy with only around 1500 clicks being what you need to win the game. This is strictly to take up less time and for review purposes. Thank you for playing. (Settings has original music if that's what you fancy, p.s only "materials" you need for this game are a functioning computer and the zip file at github.com/deyist.
